About the Municipality

Culture, nature, and heritage in one destination.

Tboli is a municipality in South Cotabato known for its natural attractions, Indigenous culture, and strong community identity.

The Municipality of Tboli was created by virtue of Presidential Decree No. 407, with Kematu designated as the seat of government. It is home to rich landscapes, upland communities, and a culture deeply connected to tradition and nature.

The Tboli people, also known as Tagabili, are known for their unique culture, oral traditions, music, rituals, and the intricate art of T'nalak weaving. Their heritage continues to inspire the identity of the municipality.

Today, Tboli welcomes visitors to discover its lakes, caves, waterfalls, weaving centers, and cultural communities through responsible and meaningful tourism.
